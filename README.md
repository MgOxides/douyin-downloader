3. Copy the video URL from Douyin or TikTok.

## ⚙️ How It Works (In Simple Terms)

douyin-downloader uses a mix of tools behind the scenes:

- It downloads the video using Playwright, which automates the web actions required to get videos from Douyin or TikTok.
- Whisper, an AI transcription system, listens to the video’s audio and turns speech into text.
- The text is divided into small, manageable parts automatically.
- The transcription output is formatted as Markdown, which saves your text cleanly with headings, breaks, and paragraphs.

All you do is provide the video link, and the app takes care of the rest.
